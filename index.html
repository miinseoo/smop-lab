About SMOP

Solving Complex
Transportation Problems

The Smart Mobility Operations and Planning (SMOP) Lab at Korea University focuses on developing rigorous mathematical models and intelligent systems that address real-world challenges in transportation networks, logistics, and urban mobility. We combine operations research, machine learning, and large-scale data analysis to create practical solutions.

📐Mathematical Optimization & Operations Research
🤖Machine Learning & AI for Transportation
📊Big Data Analytics & Travel Behavior
🚌Public Transit Planning & Network Design
