About SMOP

The Smart Mobility Operations and Planning (SMOP) Lab at Korea University develops rigorous mathematical models and intelligent systems to address real-world challenges in transportation networks, logistics, and urban mobility. We combine operations research, machine learning, and large-scale data analysis to create practical solutions.
